The application is designed to be user-friendly and secure to keep private information confidential. The purpose of this application is to help its users keep their life on track, and stay organized and on top of their busy, hectic lives.
...or at least that's what we wrote in the user manual. I worked on this project with three other students. I wrote the app and they wrote most of the documentation.

Neural Network Fighting AI
 2016

I made a fighting game in Unreal Engine 4 and trained the AI using a neural network. Training data was obtained by getting two people to play the game and collecting all the actions from the winning player. I wrote an external neural network training program that took the training data, trained a neural network how to win the fighting game, and save a data file containing the resultant neural network. Finally, the neural network data was used in the fighting game to control the AI player.
